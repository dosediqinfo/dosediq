Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2026

Overview

DosedIQ ("we", "our", "us") operates educational video channels on YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in connection with our services.

Information We Collect

DosedIQ does not collect personal information from viewers. Our videos are published on third-party platforms (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok), each governed by their own privacy policies.

YouTube API Usage

Our application uses the YouTube API Services to upload and manage video content on our own YouTube channel. By using YouTube API Services, we are bound by the Google Privacy Policy.

Data Accessed

  • We access only our own YouTube channel data for the purpose of uploading videos, setting thumbnails, and managing our published content.
  • We do not access, collect, or store any viewer or third-party user data through the YouTube API.

Data Storage

  • OAuth tokens for our own YouTube channel authentication are stored locally and securely on our systems.
  • No user data from viewers or third parties is stored.

Third-Party Platforms

When you view our content on YouTube, Instagram, or TikTok, those platforms may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • YouTube / Google Privacy Policy
  • Instagram / Meta Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Privacy Policy

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
